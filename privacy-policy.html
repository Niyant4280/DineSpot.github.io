Welcome to Dine Spot’s Privacy Policy. We are committed to protecting your data and respecting your privacy.

Data Protection

We implement strong security measures to protect your data. All information is encrypted and securely stored. However, no security measure is foolproof, so we encourage you to keep your passwords secure.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@dinespot.com.
